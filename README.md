AKAN NAME GENERATOR
The Akan name generator webpage is used mainly to find out what your name would be in the indigenous community of the Akan community.
The webpage helps one to know their name without the use of traditional methods such as using a calendar to check the day you were born and try to figure out your name.
PROPERTIES 
The first thing you will notice is the background image of the webpage.
The image has been added to make one feel the setting of the page.
To make you feel inspired that you are about to find out your name in Akan.
When you enter the page you will first be required to enter you date of birth in the format provided in the page.
